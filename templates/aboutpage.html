About RISHAB'S FOOD COURT

RISHAB'S FOOD COURT is located near the admin block in SSN College of Engineering. It offers a wide variety of dishes to cater to different tastes.

The food court provides the following meal timings:

    Breakfast: 7:30 am to 10:45 am

    Lunch: 11:00 am to 2:00 pm

    Juice and other items: 9:00 am to 9:00 pm

    Dinner: 7:00 pm to 9:00 pm

About SSN College of Engineering

Sri Sivasubramaniya Nadar (SSN), founded by Mr. Shiv Nadar, Chairman, HCL Technologies, stands out as a premier center of higher learning with a mission of pursuing excellence in education and research. The institutions, with their diverse and dynamic community of students offer a distinctive combination of some of the finest graduate, undergraduate and research programs, accomplished faculty, world class facilities and a residential campus set on a sprawling 250 acres of sylvan surroundings.
